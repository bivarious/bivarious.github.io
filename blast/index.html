Blast Games

Crush blocks, solve puzzles and unlock new levels.

Have a blast with Blast Games!

Highly addictive puzzle game. Crush blocks, solve puzzles and unlock new levels. Master your strategic thinking and logical reasoning skills. Game difficulty increases gradually.

    How to Play:
  • Match and crush two or more connected blocks of the same color to clear the board and advance to a new level.
  • To solve puzzles and unlock new levels - collect a designated amount of blocks in appointed colors. You have a limited amount of moves per level.
  • Think though and plan your future steps ahead.
  • Use hammers to remove unwanted single blocks.
  • Start with five free hammers. More hammers are available for purchase.
  • Try to blast more blocks to earn a higher score and get on the leaderboard.
  • Compete with players worldwide.
